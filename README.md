# UAE Business Setup FAQ

Frequently asked questions answered by **Xillion Group UAE**.

## Can a foreigner own a UAE company?

Foreign ownership is available for many mainland and free zone activities, subject to applicable regulations.

## Should I choose mainland or free zone?

The right option depends on your activity, target market, office requirements, visas, banking needs and expansion plans.

## How long does company formation take?

Timelines vary depending on the jurisdiction, activity, approvals, documents and immigration requirements.

## Do I need a UAE residence visa?

Not every shareholder must hold a residence visa, but residency may support banking, leasing and daily business operations.

## Can Xillion guarantee a bank account?

No. Banks make independent decisions based on compliance, risk and due diligence.

## Does every UAE company need Corporate Tax registration?

Obligations depend on the legal and tax status of the business. Professional advice should be obtained where necessary.

## Can I sponsor my family?

Eligible UAE residents may sponsor qualifying family members, subject to immigration requirements.

## Are setup prices fixed?

No. Costs vary by jurisdiction, activity, visa allocation, office requirement and government approvals.
